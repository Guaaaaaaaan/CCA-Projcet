Takina Inoue

Takina Inoue (井ノ上 たきな Inoue Takina) is the deuteragonist of the original anime series, Lycoris Recoil. She was previously the partner of Fuki Harukawa, but due to disobeying orders on a mission, she was transferred to Café LycoReco, where she is now partnered with Chisato Nishikigi.
